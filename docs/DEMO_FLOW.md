# 90-Second Demo Flow

## Goal

Show that AfterLife is not just another wallet tool. It is the first on-chain protocol that treats death verification with gravity, reversibility, and emotional clarity.

## Walkthrough Script

1. 0s-15s: Landing page
   Long pause on the hero headline.
   Say: "Death is not the end. It is the moment your legacy begins."
   Then move slowly to the `$140B` lost-to-crypto-deaths statistic and ask:
   "How many fortunes have already vanished because the owner died without a plan?"

2. 15s-30s: Owner dashboard
   Open `Sarah's Active Will`.
   Press the large `I'm Alive` button.
   Say: "AfterLife begins with proof of life. The protocol assumes nothing. It asks, gently, whether you're still here."

3. 30s-75s: Beneficiary verification
   Switch to the verification page.
   Use `John's Pending Will`.
   Submit the obituary URL.
   Let the 8-second modal play without rushing it.
   Narrate the stages:
   - "Reading the obituary"
   - "Cross-referencing legitimate sources"
   - "Examining memorial activity"
   - "AI validators reaching consensus"
   At the verdict reveal, say:
   "Even when AfterLife is confident, it does not execute immediately. It begins a 14-day grace period so mistakes can still be reversed."

4. 75s-90s: Emotional payoff
   Open `Mary's Executed Will`.
   Go to final messages.
   Reveal Lily's message.
   Say:
   "This is the real product. Not just asset transfer, but memory transfer. Not just custody, but care."

## Closing Line

"Death is inevitable. Being forgotten is not. AfterLife on GenLayer."
