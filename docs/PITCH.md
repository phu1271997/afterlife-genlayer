# AfterLife Pitch

## 60-90 Second Script

"Mircea Popescu reportedly held over two billion dollars in Bitcoin. In 2021, he drowned in Costa Rica. If those keys died with him, that fortune is effectively gone forever. And he is not unique. Chainalysis estimates that a huge share of Bitcoin is already lost, much of it because owners died without a plan."

"At the same time, Cerulli estimates that 68 trillion dollars will pass between generations by 2045. Yet most crypto holders still have no inheritance protocol. Traditional wills are slow, expensive, and disconnected from self-custodied assets. Dead-man's switches are easy to fake. Oracles cannot decide whether an obituary is real."

"AfterLife is the first AI-verified digital will protocol built on GenLayer. Owners create a will, designate beneficiaries, and leave final messages. They periodically prove they are still alive. If they miss check-ins, beneficiaries can trigger verification. GenLayer validators read obituary pages, cross-reference memorial evidence, and use AI consensus to decide whether the death claim is real, suspicious, or inconclusive."

"If death is confirmed, AfterLife does not execute immediately. It begins a 14-day reversible grace period. The owner can return. Family can dispute. Only then does the protocol unlock assets and final messages."

"This is only possible on GenLayer, because it is the first blockchain where contracts can read the web and reason about subjective real-world events inside consensus."

"Our business model is simple: one-time will creation fees, annual maintenance, and a small execution fee. With more than 420 million crypto users globally, even modest adoption creates a major category."

"AfterLife turns inevitability into infrastructure. Try it now. Create your will. Live forever in your loved ones' memory."

## Five-Slide Outline

1. Problem
   `$140B` in crypto lost to deaths, `$68T` generational transfer, no decentralized inheritance layer

2. Why current solutions fail
   Probate is slow, dead-man's switches are brittle, oracles cannot judge authenticity

3. AfterLife live demo
   Create will, proof-of-life, AI verification, grace period, final messages

4. Why GenLayer
   `web.render` + `exec_prompt` + validator consensus make death verification possible

5. Business and future
   Consumer estate product, exchange integrations, legal bridges, legacy archive network

## Business Model

- `$50-$200` per will creation in a production consumer product
- `$5` annual maintenance or active monitoring plan
- `1%` execution fee for released assets
- B2B estate planning integrations for exchanges and custodians

- Even 1% penetration creates a very large estate-planning niche
